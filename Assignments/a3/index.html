Sandwich


Ingredients: 
            4 slices bread (any)
            2 tsps butter or ghee 
            ½ tsp cumin or jeera.
            1 tsp garlic chopped or ½ tsp ginger garlic paste.
            1 pinch turmeric or haldi.
            2 green chilies chopped or ½ tsp chili powder.
            1 cup mixed veggies heaped, (carrot, onion, capsicum, corn, beans)
